现在你可以这样用

一、启动

交互模式：

`python -m src.app.main`

二、基础命令

- `help`
- `status`
- `frame`
- `frame full truth`
- `step`
- `step 3`
- `step 3 truth`
- `step 3 player`
- `events`
- `events 10`
- `events full truth`
- `events region region_04 12 full truth`
- `events faction civ_01_faction_01 10 brief player`
- `save`
- `reset`
- `quit`

三、观察命令

- `watch region region_04`
- `watch region region_04 full truth`
- `watch character char_p_01 full player`
- `watch civ civ_01 full truth`
- `watch faction civ_01_faction_01 full player`
- `watch relic relic_01 full truth`
- `watch project project_01 full truth`
- `watch supply supply_01 full truth`
- `watch dynamic dyn_0001 full truth`
- `watch dynamic dyn_0001 full player`
- `watch region region_04 full truth propose=dynamic`
- `watch region region_04 full truth apply=dynamic`
- `targets dynamic`
- `targets dynamic 5`
- `audit proposals`
- `audit proposals 5`
- `audit proposals summary`
- `audit proposals summary 20`

四、focus 过滤示例

- `watch civ civ_01 full player focus=summary`
- `watch civ civ_01 full truth focus=relations`
- `watch faction civ_01_faction_01 full truth focus=history`
- `watch character char_p_01 full player focus=front`
- `events 20 full truth focus=project`
- `events region region_04 12 full truth focus=anomaly`

五、调试命令

- `debug llm`

说明：

- `truth` 为系统真相视角
- `player` 为玩家观察视角
- `brief/full` 控制输出密度
- `focus=...` 用于限制观察块，减少噪声
